Butter Paneer Masala

Posted by: Kranthi Erra | Date: May 1, 2025 | Category: Indian/Curries

Ingredients

  • 2 Paneer cubed - 1lb
  • 4 Roma Toamtoes
  • 2 Yellow Onions
  • Handful Cashew Nuts(broken or full)
  • 2 Whole Cardamom
  • 1 inch Cinnamon stick
  • 1/2 tsp Ginger Paste
  • 1 tsp Garlic Paste
  • 2 tsp Garam Masala
  • 1/2 Stick Salted Butter
  • 1 tsp Red Chili Powder
  • 2 tsp Salt
  • Chopped Handful of Cilantro

Instructions

  1. In a pan, add roughly chopped tomatoes, roughly chopped onions, cashew nuts, ginger paste, garlic paste, Cardamom, Cinnamon and cook till they're soft and wait until they're cooled down or little less hot.
  2. Now, the cooked onion and tomatoes should be blend into a nice and creamy paste. Keep the paste ready, take a pan and add butter, let it melt. Add this paste to the melted butter and now add salt, chili powder and garam masala to this paste and cook for about 5 mins with lid.
  3. Meanwhile it's cooking, dice paneer into cubes and once the paste is cooked for 5 mins add the cubed paneer and cook for a couple of mins and add chopped cilantro on top and you're done.
  4. Add some fresh cream on if you like decorating your plate.
  5. Enjoy with Naan or Rice or Both.
