About Me

🙋‍♂️ Hi, I’m Kranthi Kumar Erra — but people call me Kranthi or if you don't know how to say my name? you can call me bro. I’m a believer in Christ Jesus, musician, pastor and home chef originally from Hyderabad, India, now living in the Oswego, New York. I created this space—kranthierra.com—as a place to share the things that stir my soul: faith, music, words, and a good plate of food.

Each part of this website will show the journey God has taken me on. Whether I’m writing about faith, arranging a song of worship, or posting a recipe that reminds me of home, it all flows from one place: a deep desire to glorify God and bless others.

✝️ Rooted in Faith

Romans 8:1 reminds me every day of the freedom we have in Christ: "Therefore, there is now no condemnation for those who are in Christ Jesus."

I believe God gives each of us unique gifts—not for our own spotlight, but to serve others and reflect His light in a world that’s often searching for truth in the dark.

🎵 Where My Passions Meet Purpose

Music has been part of my life for as long as I can remember. when I first started to learn and play in 2012, my teacher said, if you learn from you promise me that you'll not play for silly purposes but only for Christ, I did not in the beginnning, but now I do. I will also be arranging music sheets here.

Writing helps me process and pray. Through blogs, I share life lessons, scriptural reflections, and encouragement from personal experiences. It’s like having a nice cup of chai(it's not chai-tea) with a good friend but in digital form.

Cooking is where creativity meets comfort. Some of the best conversations I’ve had have been around the dinner table. I share favorite recipes here—simple, soulful dishes that connect me to my roots and bring people together.
