Bridging Ancient Faith with Modern Life.

I believe that plating a Hyderabadi Biryani and arranging a worship song are similar acts—both require patience, layering, and a deep desire to bring people together.

Hello, I’m Kranthi. A believer, musician, and storyteller originally from Hyderabad, India, now finding new rhythms in Oswego, New York.

Rooted in Faith

Romans 8:1 reminds me every day of the freedom we have in Christ. I didn't always see it this way. Growing up, faith was a tradition. Today, it is my anchor. I believe God gives each of us unique gifts—not for our own spotlight, but to serve others and reflect His light in a world that’s often searching for truth.

The Sound of Worship

Music has been part of my life since 2012. My teacher once made me promise that I would not play for "silly purposes" but only for Christ. That promise stuck. Whether I am arranging sheets or playing in private, every note is an offering.

The Art of Hospitality

Cooking is where creativity meets comfort. Some of the best conversations I’ve had have been around the dinner table over a plate of Butter Paneer. I share recipes here not just to feed the stomach, but to connect with the soul.
